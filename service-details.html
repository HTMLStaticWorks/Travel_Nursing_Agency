Luxury Housing Assistance

Live Comfortably While You Work

At NurseSync, we believe that your home should be a sanctuary after a long shift. That's why we offer "HomeSync Luxury Housing" for all our travel nurses.

Unlike other agencies that provide minimal stipends, we provide fully furnished, move-in ready apartments in high-end developments close to your assigned facility.

What's Included?

  • All Utilities (Electricity, Water, Trash)
  • High-Speed Wireless Internet
  • Premium Linens and Modern Furniture
  • Washer and Dryer in Unit
  • Pet-Friendly Options Available
